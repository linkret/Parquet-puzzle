Parquet Puzzle
Rules & Scoring
RULES: Fill the grid with numbers from 1 to 9 so that each colored segment bordered by a thick line contains 3 different numbers. The number in the middle must be the largest. Same digits must not touch anywhere, not even diagonally.

SCORING: All numbers in the grid are added up. Your final result is reduced by the time spent in seconds divided by 10.
